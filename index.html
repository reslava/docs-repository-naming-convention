Quick Guide

✅ Best Practices:

  • • Use lowercase only
  • • Separate words with hyphens
  • • Keep it 3-5 words total
  • • Be descriptive yet concise
  • • Avoid special characters

❌ Avoid:

  • • Version numbers in name
  • • Underscores or spaces
  • • PascalCase or camelCase
  • • Generic names (app, project)
  • • Personal names
